Akan Names

The Akan people use the Kwa language group's system of timekeeping, which is based on a six-day week , with the seventh day inclusive. In Ashanti homes, Sunday-born males and females are named Kwasi or Kwesi and Akosua respectively, Monday-born are Kwadwo or Kojo, Adjoa or Adwoa,Tuesday-born are Kwabena or Kobi, Abena , Wednesday-born Kwaku or Kweku, Akua , Thursday-born Yaw, Yaa , Friday-born Kofi, Afia or Afua and Saturday-born Kwame, Ama..
